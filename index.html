Why We fight?

The United States of America's freedom depends on the army, navy, marines, and air force. The army, being the first made of the bunch, is the key in land defence, and the United States Army will defend America until our last drop of blood.

Values Of the Army.

Honor is the first value. Honor is not given, it is earned by hard work and dedication. The second value is professionalism. In the Army, we do not fool around, because our freedom depends on us. Respect is the third value. No matter the rank, size, or age, you should always respect others.
